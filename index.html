Scout's Haven

A Tale of Desert Survival and Community

A Novel

When their ancient mesquite tree begins to die, three unlikely companions—William the anxious porcupine, Gil the patient Gila monster, and Sarel the observant coachwhip snake—must leave everything they know to search for a new home in the harsh Sonoran Desert.

Their desperate journey will test every assumption, forge unexpected bonds, and ultimately transform them from refugees into founders of a thriving community built on curiosity, cooperation, and courage.
